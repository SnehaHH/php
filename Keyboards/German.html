German Keyboard

The German characters that are already available on a default English keyboard can be accessed in the same way as a default English keyboard. The special characters from the German script such as ö, ä, ü, ß, etc., can be accessed by pressing the keys according to the following guide:

  • =a for ä
  • =o for ö
  • =u for ü
  • =s for ß
  • =A for Ä
  • =O for Ö
  • =U for Ü

You can also type these characters by clicking on their buttons from the on-screen keyboard. The additional special characters such as punctuations can be accessed directly using your keyboard or the on-screen virtual keyboard. Click on the desired buttons or press the letter keys on your keyboard to type. You can use Caps Lock or Shift to type capital letters. You can access special characters by pressing Shift + character key or clicking on Shift and then the character button. Remember to paste the text for the spell checkers, as entered text will be copied to clipboard :)
